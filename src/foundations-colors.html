Colors

In this repository, the colors have been chosen based on the Exeen brand, with the aim of maintaining visual consistency with the brand identity. To ensure accessibility, we have only modified the Saturation (S) and Lightness (L) values of the colors, keeping the Hue (H) value constant. This adjustment ensures that the colors are easily distinguishable for all users, adhering to accessibility guidelines without compromising the original appearance of the brand.
